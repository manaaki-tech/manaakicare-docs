# Welcome to Manaaki Central

Manaaki Central is a comprehensive case management platform designed for community services in New Zealand. It helps organizations manage clients, referrals, service delivery, and case documentation efficiently.

## What is Manaaki Central?

Manaaki Central provides tools for:

- **<Term path="serviceUser" plural /> Management** - Search, view, and manage client information and family relationships
- **<Term path="referral" plural /> Processing** - Create and track referrals through the intake workflow
- **<Term path="serviceEpisode" plural />** - Manage time-bound service delivery to clients
- **<Term path="activity" plural /> Recording** - Document visits, phone calls, case notes, and other interactions
- **Role-Based Access** - Different views and permissions for <Term path="caseWorker" plural />, supervisors, and program managers

## Who Uses Manaaki Central?

Manaaki Central supports different user roles:

- **<Term path="caseWorker" plural />** - Front-line staff managing client relationships and service delivery
- **Supervisors** - Team leads reviewing work and approving referrals
- **Program Managers** - Administrators overseeing program performance and reporting

Each role has access to different features and dashboards tailored to their responsibilities.
