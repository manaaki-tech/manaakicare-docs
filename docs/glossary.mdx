# Glossary

Common terms and definitions used in ManaakiCare.

:::note Work in Progress
This documentation is being developed. Check back soon for updates.
:::

## A

**Activity**
: A record of work performed or interaction with a client during a service episode.

**Assessment**
: A formal evaluation of client needs, strengths, and service requirements.

## C

**Case Worker**
: Front-line staff member who manages direct client relationships and service delivery.

**Client**
: An individual receiving services through ManaakiCare (also called a family member or service user).

## E

**Episode**
: See Service Episode.

## F

**Family**
: A group of related individuals tracked together in the system.

**Family Member**
: An individual within a family unit (may or may not be receiving services).

## R

**Referral**
: The formal intake process for bringing a client into service, documenting needs and initiating service delivery.

**Referral Source**
: The organisation or individual who referred the client for services.

## S

**Service Episode**
: A time-bound period during which services are actively delivered to a client.

**Service User**
: Another term for a client - an individual receiving services.

**Supervisor**
: A team lead who oversees case workers, reviews work, and approves referrals.
