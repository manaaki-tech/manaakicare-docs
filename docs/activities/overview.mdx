## What are Activities?

Activities are records of interactions and work performed with or on behalf of clients during service episodes.

## Activity Types

ManaakiCare supports various activity types:

- **Home Visits** - In-person visits at client location
- **Office Visits** - Client visits to your office
- **Phone Calls** - Telephone conversations
- **Emails** - Email correspondence
- **Case Notes** - General documentation
- **Assessments** - Formal assessments
- **Referrals Out** - External referrals
