## What are <Term path="activity" plural />?

<Term path="activity" plural /> are records of interactions and work performed with or on behalf of clients during <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural />.

## <Term path="activity" /> Types

ManaakiCare supports various <Term path="activity" /> types:

- **Home Visits** - In-person visits at client location
- **Office Visits** - Client visits to your office
- **Phone Calls** - Telephone conversations
- **Emails** - Email correspondence
- **Case Notes** - General documentation
- **Assessments** - Formal assessments
- **<Term path="referral" plural /> Out** - External <Term path="referral" plural />
