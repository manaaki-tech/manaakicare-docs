# <Term path="caseWorker" /> Role

Understand the <Term path="caseWorker" /> role, including <Term path="caseload" /> management, <Term path="activity" /> recording, and service delivery responsibilities.

## Overview

<Term path="caseWorker" plural /> are front-line staff who manage direct <Term path="serviceUser" /> relationships and service delivery. They work with assigned <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural />, record <Term path="activity" plural /> and case notes, and ensure <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> receive timely follow-up and support. Their dashboard — **"My <Term path="caseload" />"** — provides a personal view of their active <Term path="cases" /> and pending follow-ups.

## Key Responsibilities

### <Term path="caseload" /> Management
- Managing assigned <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> from commencement to completion
- Tracking active <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> and their current status
- Ensuring timely service commencement for newly assigned <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural />
- Monitoring and completing service delivery

### <Term path="serviceUser" /> Engagement
- Conducting in-person visits and telehealth sessions
- Maintaining regular contact with <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> (tracked by the system)
- Building and maintaining <Term path="serviceUser" /> relationships
- Identifying and responding to <Term path="serviceUser" /> needs

### <Term path="activity" /> Recording
- Recording all <Term path="serviceUser" /> interactions (visits, phone calls, telehealth)
- Writing case notes and documenting observations
- Setting follow-up dates for future actions
- Assessing and recording risk levels for <Term path="activity" plural />

### Follow-up Management
- Tracking scheduled follow-ups via the dashboard
- Addressing overdue follow-ups promptly
- Prioritizing activities based on risk level and urgency
- Rescheduling missed interactions

### Documentation
- Maintaining accurate <Term path="serviceUser" /> records
- Uploading supporting documents
- Recording <Term path="activity" /> outcomes (completed, no-show, rescheduled, etc.)
- Keeping <Term path="serviceEpisode" /> records up to date

## Data Visibility

<Term path="caseWorker" plural /> have a **personal scope** — they can only see data directly assigned to them:

| Data Type | Scope |
|-----------|-------|
| <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> | Only episodes where assigned as staff |
| <Term path="activity" plural /> | Only <Term path="activity" plural /> within assigned <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> |
| <Term path="serviceUser" /> records | Only <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> linked to assigned <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> |
| Referral counts | Only <Term path="referral" plural /> linked to assignments |

This means your dashboard shows only your workload, keeping the view focused and uncluttered.

## Dashboard Features

The <Term path="caseWorker" /> Dashboard — **"My <Term path="caseload" />"** — provides:

- **Analytics Overview** — Active <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural />, urgent/crisis count, follow-ups due, total <Term path="referral" plural />
- **My Active <Term path="cases" />** — All <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> assigned to you with status and actions

## Workflow Overview

### Daily Workflow

```
Check Dashboard Metrics
  → Address Overdue Follow-ups (red badges)
  → Process Urgent/Crisis Items
  → Conduct Scheduled Visits & Sessions
  → Record Activities & Case Notes
  → Set Follow-up Dates
  → Review Upcoming Follow-ups
```

### <Term path="serviceEpisode" /> Lifecycle

```
Assigned (awaiting_commencement)
  → Active (service delivery in progress)
  → Completed / Closed / Discharged
```

## Best Practices

### Prioritization
- Check **Urgent/Crisis** count first thing each day
- Address **overdue follow-ups** (red badges) before anything else
- Process **follow-ups due today** next
- Then work through routine <Term path="caseload" />

### <Term path="serviceUser" /> Contact
- Maintain regular contact — your supervisor is alerted when <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> aren't contacted for 5+ days
- Record every interaction as an <Term path="activity" />
- Set follow-up dates to ensure no <Term path="serviceUser" /> falls through the cracks

### <Term path="activity" /> Recording
- Record <Term path="activity" plural /> promptly after each <Term path="serviceUser" /> interaction
- Set appropriate risk levels (None, Medium, High, Critical)
- Always set a follow-up date when future action is needed
- Mark <Term path="activity" /> outcomes accurately (completed, no-show, rescheduled, etc.)

### Documentation Quality
- Write clear, concise case notes
- Include relevant observations and <Term path="serviceUser" /> feedback
- Upload supporting documents when available
- Keep <Term path="serviceEpisode" /> records current
