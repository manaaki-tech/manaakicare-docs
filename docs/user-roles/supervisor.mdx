# Supervisor Role

Understand the supervisor role, including team management, <Term path="referral" /> allocation, and oversight capabilities.

## Overview

Supervisors lead teams of <Term path="caseWorker" plural />, manage incoming <Term path="referral" /> requests, and ensure <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> receive timely and quality service delivery. They serve as the bridge between program management and frontline <Term path="caseWorker" plural />.

## Key Responsibilities

### <Term path="referral" plural /> Request Management
- Reviewing incoming <Term path="referral" /> service requests
- Accepting or rejecting <Term path="referral" plural /> with documented reasons
- Allocating accepted <Term path="referral" plural /> to team members
- Ensuring timely processing of urgent and priority <Term path="referral" plural />

### Team Oversight
- Monitoring <Term path="caseWorker" /> <Term path="activity" /> and engagement
- Tracking <Term path="serviceUser" /> contact frequency (flagging <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> not contacted in 5+ days)
- Reviewing <Term path="activity" plural /> / <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> ratio for team productivity
- Managing workload distribution across team members

### Service Delivery Monitoring
- Tracking active <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> under their supervision
- Monitoring pending service dispatches
- Ensuring <Term path="referral" plural /> progress through the workflow
- Following up on delayed or stalled <Term path="referral" plural />

### Quality Assurance
- Reviewing <Term path="referral" /> assessments for completeness
- Ensuring documentation standards are maintained
- Providing guidance on complex cases
- Escalating issues to program managers when needed

| Action | Permission |
|--------|------------|
| View <Term path="referral" /> requests for supervised services | Yes |
| Accept <Term path="referral" /> requests | Yes |
| Reject <Term path="referral" /> requests (with reason) | Yes |
| Allocate <Term path="referral" plural /> to team members | Yes |
| View supervised <Term path="caseWorker" plural />' records | Yes |
| View active <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> | Yes |
| View team <Term path="activity" plural /> | Yes |
| View <Term path="serviceUser" /> information | Yes |

| Access settings | No (Admin only) |

## Data Visibility

Supervisors can see:
- **Their own records** - personal <Term path="activity" plural /> and assignments
- **Supervised staff records** - all records belonging to <Term path="caseWorker" plural /> they supervise
- **Organisation-level <Term path="referral" plural />** - <Term path="referral" /> requests assigned to their services

This scoped visibility ensures supervisors have full oversight of their team without accessing data outside their responsibility.

## Workflow Overview

### <Term path="referral" /> Request Processing

```
Referral Dispatched → Service Request Created → Supervisor Reviews
    → Accept (allocate to team member) → Service Delivery
    → Reject (with documented reason)
```

### Supervision Hierarchy

```
Program Manager
  └── Supervisor
        └── Case Worker 1
        └── Case Worker 2
        └── Case Worker 3
```

## Best Practices

### Prioritization
- Process **Critical** and **Urgent** <Term path="referral" /> requests immediately
- Review the Pending Requests card at the start of each day
- Monitor the <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> Needing Contact section daily

### Team Management
- Distribute workload evenly across <Term path="caseWorker" plural />
- Use the <Term path="activity" plural /> / <Term path="serviceEpisode" plural /> ratio to gauge team engagement
- Follow up when <Term path="serviceUser" plural /> haven't been contacted in 5+ days

### Communication
- Provide clear notes when allocating <Term path="referral" plural /> to team members
- Document reasons when rejecting <Term path="referral" plural />
- Escalate complex cases to program managers promptly

### Timeliness
- Aim to process new <Term path="referral" /> requests within 24 hours
- Follow up on <Term path="referral" plural /> pending service dispatch
- Monitor the "Days" column for overdue <Term path="serviceUser" /> contacts
